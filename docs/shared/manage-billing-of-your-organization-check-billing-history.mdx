Information about outstanding payments and billing receipts is available from the {/* {book['console']} */}.

To check your billing history:

1. {/* {book['login']} */}

1. Select the user icon on the header bar and choose **Billing** from the menu.

1. On the **History** page, select the invoice number for a detailed PDF.
