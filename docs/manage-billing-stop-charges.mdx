Got a deployment you no longer need and don't want to be charged for any longer? Simply delete it.

<DocCallOut title="Important" color="warning">
**All data is lost.** Billing for usage is by the hour and any outstanding charges for usage before you deleted the deployment will still appear on your next bill.
</DocCallOut>

To stop being charged for a deployment:

1. Log in to the ((es)) Serverless Console.

2. Find the name of the deployment you no longer need and click on it.

3. Click **Actions** then select **Delete deployment** and confirm the deletion.
